Events conducted by us

ISTE Student’s Chapter, Manipal is MIT, Manipal’s largest Multi-Domain Club, i.e, it is not specifically oriented to cater to only Computer Science students or Electronic Students, rather it believes in development for all the students who become a part of the club.

ISTE organizes various events throughout an academic year. The workshops and events organized by the club prove very usefull for the students of MIT and they get to learn a lot. A common feedback received by the club is that they organize very informative workshops and interesting events. ISTE organizes various workshops such as Intro to GIT, Python: basic to advance, OpenCV and much more. Apart from workshops ISTE has its own Tech Week.This is club’s flagship event of even semester. The TechWeek brings in all the different domains of engineering into a series of events. TechWeek has many events like “Watt Up”, “ExQUIZite”, “Clueless”, “Unlockathon” and “Clash of Codes”. The TechWeek had a participation of over 1200 people including both Online and Offline event. ISTE also has a lot to offer even if you're not in the campus.In summer and winter breaks ISTE organizes summer and winter school which is a crash course of latest technologies taught by none other than your own seniors.If all that was not enough then guess what ISTE is the only club to organize on-demand workshops.

Sundays

ISTE Manipal Student’s Chapter is the first club which keeps Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning Sundays. This weekly event has emphasis on the new technologies in the field of AI. The event covers all latest inventions like Google Duplex. The AI Sundays is very well received in participation as well as the feedbacks were positive. The club begin the first of its kind to organize such AI related event takes pride to say that its MIT, Manipal biggest multi-domain club. The AI Sunday’s are basically AI6, AI Saturday which is a worldwide event. AI6, it is a structured study group organised in 50+ cities across the globe, including Bangalore, Lagos, Toronto, Singapore and Sunnyvale. It is community-driven and free-to-attend. AI Saturdays (AI6) is catalysed by Nurture.AI. The first cycle of AI6 began in January 2018 and ended in May 2018. Over 5,000 participants from 50 cities worldwide were part of the AI6 movement.
